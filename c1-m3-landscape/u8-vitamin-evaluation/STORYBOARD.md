# U8 Storyboard — Vitamin 5 模型横评

## START
Hook: 上节课学了怎么调研模型. 今天你就是 Vitamin 的 AI PM, 要做一个真正的模型选型报告.
Preview: 5 个候选模型 + 6 个评估维度 + 交互打分 + 产出选型报告

## (1) PM 面试常问: "你选这个模型, 理由是什么?"
Observable: 面试官不会接受 "因为大家都用它" 这种回答. 你需要有数据支撑的选型理由.

## (2) 错误做法 vs 正确做法
Wrong: "选最强的" or "选最便宜的". Truth: 用 6 维度框架, 按场景需求加权.

## (3) 认识 5 个候选模型
DeepSeek V4 Flash, Qwen3.5-Plus, Kimi K2.6, Claude Haiku 4.5, Doubao-Seed-2.0

## (4) 维度 1-2: Cost + Chinese Quality
Cost comparison table. Chinese quality rankings from SuperCLUE.

## (5) 维度 3-4: Context Length + Speed
Context windows. Speed/latency data from Artificial Analysis.

## (6) 维度 5-6: Multimodal + Reasoning
Which ones support vision? Reasoning depth comparison.

## (7) 加权评分 — Vitamin 的优先级
Not all dimensions equal weight. For Vitamin: Chinese quality > Cost > Speed > Reasoning > Multimodal > Context.

## (8) Widget: Model Evaluation Board
5x6 interactive grid. Pre-loaded with suggested ratings. User can adjust. Shows weighted total. Highlight winner.

## (9) Vitamin 选型报告模板
Put it all together into a 1-page report format that PM can use in interviews.

## (10) One-liner + homework + resources
Rule: "模型选型 = 6 维度 × 加权 × 你的场景. 没有放之四海皆准的最佳."
